Welcome to the Acorn Alliance!

Deep within the heart of the East Commonlands, hidden among the ancient roots of Yggdrasil, lies the secret lair of the Squirrels of War. Our merry band of adventurers is dedicated to guarding the sacred nuts of Ratatoskr and scurrying through the lands to clear every challenge that Kunark throws our way. We raid three times a week—Sunday, Monday, and Wednesday at 8:15 PM EST—where we earn our acorns (or DKP, if you prefer) and grow stronger together. Welcome to our nutty little world!

Managing Your DKP

To join our merry band and start collecting acorns (DKP), follow these steps:

  1. Head to the DKP Creation channel in Discord.
  2. Provide the name, rank (main or alt), level, class, race, and gender of your character.
  3. Next, visit the Squirrels OpenDKP site and register an account.
  4. Log in, click on "Characters," and request access to your character.
  5. Once access is granted, log back in, go to "Characters," and claim your character.
  6. If you’re missing raid ticks (DKP credit), visit the "Missing Raid Ticks" section, find the missing tick, and request approval.

Nuts for Epics

Chasing that epic? Follow these steps to get on the tracker:

  1. Go to the Epic Tracker.
  2. Request edit access in the top right corner.
  3. Select your class tab at the bottom of the Google Sheet.
  4. Add your character in the same format as the others listed.
